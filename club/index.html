The Fashion Country Club

In many ways the Club House at Fashion captures the very essence of the Club itself.

More than just a beautiful course!

Fashion Golf Club, located in St. Charles Parish, features a public 9-hole championship golf course, all fairways are tree lined, and the greens are small. Four holes have water that comes into play. Fashion is the perfect venue for corporate and social events. Here you will find an incomparable level of personal service, normally found in the finest private clubs. Whether on the golf course or in the clubhouse, you’ll discover every member of our staff is dedicated to ensuring your experience is enjoyable and memorable.

Fashion is a great 9 hole regulation course located at the Fashion Golf & Country Club facility in Hahnville, LA. From the longest tees it offers 3,164 yards of golf for a par of 36. Fashion is a daily fee golf course with a ‘Call For Playing Privileges’ guest policy. The course contact is Stacy Francis, Owner/Manager.

Parties and Civic events

Fashion Golf Club takes pride in offering it’s spacious club house for your next party or event. Our friendly customer service and attention-to-detail will meet your every need, so you can enjoy your event.

Fashion welcomes all kinds of events from weddings, retirement parties, school and civic events. We offer complete professional event management designed to make your special event perfect. We welcome you and your guests, and promise to make your special day an enjoyable and successful experience.
