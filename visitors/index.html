Fashions Golf & Country Golf Club provides a welcoming reception to all visitors. Staff and members alike are committed to ensuring your enjoyment of both our course and the full range of facilities.

Visitors & Societies

You and your friends will find not only a fantastic course in first class condition, but equally importantly, you’ll be welcomed and will definitely find us an ideal venue for a casual game or a society event.

The Golf Course

Fast, true greens, well manicured fairways and cleverly placed bunkers all add to the charm of our challenging par 70, parkland gem. A brilliant mix of par 3 holes, driveable par 4’s and long par 5’s all add up to make us the finest course in the area.

Fashion Golf & Country Club – join us today. Everything here at the club is set up for the enjoyment and benefit of its members.

How to find us

From New Orleans

Head north on Carondelet St toward Union St. Take the 1st left onto Union Street. Turn left onto Baronne Street. Take the 2nd right onto Poydras Street. Merge onto I-10 W via the ramp on the left to Baton Rouge. Take exit 220 to merge onto I-310 S toward Boutte/Houma. Take exit 7 to merge onto LA-18 E/River Rd toward Luling. Make a U-turn at Ashton Plantation Blvd. Turn left onto Bethlehem Ln. Bethlehem Ln turns right and becomes Joe Louis Ln.

From Baton Rouge

Head south on N 49th St toward Jonah Street. Take the 1st left onto N 48th Street. Turn left onto North Street. Take the 2nd right onto N Foster Drive. Slight left onto S Foster Drive. Keep left at the fork. Turn right onto College Drive. Turn left onto the I-10/I-12 ramp to New Orleans/Hammond. Merge onto I-10 E. Take exit 220 to merge onto I-310 S toward Boutte/Houma. Take exit 7 to merge onto LA-18 E/River Rd toward Luling. Make a U-turn at Ashton Plantation Blvd. Turn left onto Bethlehem Ln. Bethlehem Ln turns right and becomes Joe Louis Ln. Joe Louis Ln.

From Avondale

Head west on Valentine Dr toward W Tish Drive. Turn left onto W Tish Drive. Take the 1st right onto US-90 W. Turn right onto Barton Ave. Turn left onto River Rd. Turn left onto Bethlehem Ln. Bethlehem Ln turns right and becomes Joe Louis Ln. Joe Louis Ln.
